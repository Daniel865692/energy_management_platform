Key Features

Real-Time Energy Monitoring

Track your energy consumption in real time and gain insights into your usage patterns. Visualize your energy usage data through interactive charts and graphs.

Energy Usage Analysis

Get a detailed analysis of your energy usage, including peak usage hours, high-consumption appliances, and trends over time. Identify areas for optimization and efficiency improvements.

Cost Tracking and Budgeting

Monitor your energy costs and set budgets to keep your expenses in check. Receive alerts when you exceed your budget and gain better control over your energy expenses.

Energy Efficiency Tips

Receive personalized energy-saving tips and recommendations based on your usage patterns. Discover practical ways to reduce energy waste and lower your environmental footprint.

Device Integration

Connect your smart devices and appliances to the software for seamless integration. Control and monitor your devices remotely, and automate energy-saving routines.
